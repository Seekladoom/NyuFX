Hints
  • Possible transformations with \t:
    • Colors: \1c, \2c, \3c, \4c, \c
    • Alpha: \1a, \2a, \3a, \4a, \alpha
    • Border: \bord, \xbord, \ybord
    • Shadow: \shad, \xshad, \yshad
    • Transformations: \fr, \frz, \frx, \fry, \fax, \fay, \fscx, \fscy
    • Blur: \be, \blur
    • Font: \fs, \fsp
    • Clips (rectangle): \clip, \iclip
  • All times are in milliseconds
  • Coordinates reach until 1/8 units (except drawing code)
  • Blur affects outline, but if there's no outline, it affects the whole object (text or shape)
  • If there's no \org defined, the ancher point of alignment is the orientation point for transformations
  • Accelerated movement can be realized by z-axis rotation with a far away \org or stretching (\fscx and \fscy transformation) an invisible shape at the beginning of dialog line.
  • Shapes and characters are sitting on the baseline, defined by the biggest shape or character in dialog line.
    Shapes with {} before and characters will be right-shifted in position by the sum of width of characters/shapes before.
    Minimum shape x/y coordinates less than zero can be used for left/top shifting. Minimum shape x/y coordinates greater than zero can be used for right/bottom shifting.
